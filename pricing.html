Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try before I buy?

Yes. Starter and Professional include a 14-day free trial. Enterprise includes 30 days. No credit card required to start.

What's included in each MCP server?

Every MCP server includes the AI model, API access, compliance templates, documentation, and ongoing updates. Browse our catalog to see all 76 servers.

Can I switch packages later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. We'll pro-rate the difference so you only pay for what you use.

What is CASA Certification?

Constitutional AI Safety Authority certification is an independent assessment of your AI governance. It's recognised across regulated industries worldwide.

Do you offer custom pricing?

Yes. For organisations needing sovereign deployment, custom SLAs, or large-volume MCP access, contact our sales team for a tailored quote.

How does COBOL Bridge work?

COBOL Bridge provides a translation layer between your legacy COBOL systems and modern AI workflows. It analyses, monitors, and migrates code while maintaining compliance.
