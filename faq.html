Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CSGA Global, our MCP platform, pricing, and certifications.

What is CSGA Global? +
CSGA Global (Cyber Security Global Alliance) is the world's first AI governance and Model Context Protocol (MCP) platform. We provide professional tools, training, and certification for organisations implementing responsible AI systems.
What are MCPs? +
Model Context Protocols (MCPs) are standardised integration tools that connect AI models to external systems — databases, APIs, cloud services, and security tools. CSGA Global offers 70 MCPs across Free, Pro, and Enterprise tiers.
Is the Free tier really free? +
Yes. Our Free tier includes 12 MCPs with community support, basic API access, and self-paced MOOCs — all at no cost. No credit card required to get started.
What does Pro include? +
Pro (£49/month or £470/year) adds 12 advanced MCPs, priority support, advanced analytics, team collaboration features, and access to professional certification tracks.
What is Enterprise pricing? +
Enterprise (£199/month or £1,910/year) includes all 70 MCPs, dedicated support, custom integrations, SLA guarantees, SSO/SAML, compliance reporting, and white-label options.
Can I buy individual MCPs? +
Yes. Individual MCPs are available at £9/month (Pro) or £29/month (Enterprise). We also offer a 5-MCP bundle at £69/month. Annual discounts of 20% are available.
What certifications do you offer? +
We offer 6 professional certification tracks: CSOAI Certified Professional, MCP Developer, AI Governance Specialist, Cyber Defence Analyst, Enterprise Security Architect, and CASA Compliance Officer.
Are the MOOCs accredited? +
Our 36 MOOCs are designed to CSOAI standards and align with ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, and EU AI Act frameworks. Certification exams are independently assessed.
How does CSGA relate to CSOAI? +
CSGA Global is the commercial platform of the CSOAI Group (Cyber Security of Organizational AI). CSOAI develops the standards; CSGA delivers the tools and training.
Do you support government/defence use? +
Yes. Our Enterprise tier includes CA-30 compliance, DSRB-aligned security controls, and specialised defence MCPs. Contact us for government pricing and deployment options.
What support is available? +
Free tier: community forums. Pro: priority email support (24h response). Enterprise: dedicated account manager, phone support, and custom SLA.
How do I get started? +
Visit our Pricing page, choose your tier, and sign up. Free tier is instant. Pro and Enterprise include a guided onboarding session.
