Common Questions

Quick answers to help you get started.

Do I need a paid plan to get started?

No. The Community tier is completely free and gives you access to documentation, community forums, and basic resources. Upgrade anytime when you need more.

Can I try paid plans before committing?

Yes. Starter and Professional memberships include a 14-day free trial. Enterprise plans include a 30-day trial. Cancel anytime during the trial at no cost.

What currencies do you accept?

All prices are in GBP (£). Stripe handles currency conversion automatically if you're paying from another currency.

Can I combine membership with CASA or MCP products?

Absolutely. Every product works independently. Higher membership tiers include discounts on CASA certifications and MCP bundles.

How do I get support?

Community members get forum access. Starter and above get email support. Professional includes priority support, and Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager.
