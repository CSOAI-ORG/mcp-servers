Accessibility Statement

>

Last updated: February 2026

1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

MEOK AI Labs is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all visitors with disabilities. We aim to provide a website that is compliant with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. This Accessibility Statement describes our accessibility initiatives and how users can request assistance.

2. Accessibility Features

2.1 Keyboard Navigation

Our website is designed to be fully navigable using keyboard controls alone. All interactive elements can be accessed using the Tab key, Enter key, and other standard keyboard navigation methods. Skip-to-content links are available to bypass repetitive navigation elements.

2.2 Screen Reader Support

We use semantic HTML and ARIA labels to ensure compatibility with screen readers including NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver. Images include descriptive alternative text (alt text) to convey meaning to users relying on assistive technology.

2.3 Color Contrast and Visual Design

Our website meets WCAG AA standards for color contrast ratios, ensuring text is readable for users with low vision or color blindness. We avoid relying solely on color to convey information, using supplementary visual indicators where necessary.

2.4 Responsive and Flexible Design

The website is designed to be responsive and flexible, adapting seamlessly to different screen sizes and orientations. Users can adjust text size and zoom levels without breaking the layout or losing functionality.

2.5 Captions and Transcripts

Where video content is provided, we include captions for all spoken dialogue and sound effects. Audio content is accompanied by transcripts whenever possible.

3. Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some third-party content and external links may not meet our accessibility standards. We are working with our partners to improve accessibility across our ecosystem.

Some PDF documents on our website may have varying levels of accessibility. We are committed to converting important documents to accessible formats upon request.

4. Accessibility Standards and Conformance

We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. Our accessibility efforts include:

  • Regular accessibility audits and testing
  • Training for our development team on accessible design practices
  • Continuous improvement of accessibility features
  • Compliance with relevant accessibility laws and regulations

5.1 Expected Response Time

We aim to respond to accessibility inquiries within 5 business days. We will work with you to resolve accessibility issues promptly and may provide alternative accessible formats of content if needed.

6. Accessibility Features for Specific Needs

6.1 Dyslexia Support

We use clear, sans-serif fonts and appropriate line spacing to support readers with dyslexia. We recommend users with dyslexia consider using specialized reading software in conjunction with our website.

6.2 Motor Accessibility

Our website supports voice control and keyboard navigation for users with motor disabilities. All interactive elements are appropriately sized to be easily clickable.

6.3 Cognitive Accessibility

We use clear language, logical structure, and consistent navigation to support users with cognitive disabilities. We provide explanations for technical terms and avoid unnecessary jargon where possible.

7. Third-Party Content and External Links

While we strive to ensure all linked external websites are accessible, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of third-party content. If you encounter accessibility issues with linked content, please contact us and we will do our best to assist you.

8. Accessibility Statement Updates

We regularly review and update this Accessibility Statement to reflect improvements and changes to our website. Last updated: February 2026.

9. Legal Framework

This Accessibility Statement reflects our commitment to compliance with applicable accessibility legislation, including the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), the Web Accessibility Directive, and similar regulations in other jurisdictions.
