1. Introduction

MEOK AI Labs ("we", "us", "our", or "Company") operates the MEOK AI Labs website and services. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our services.

2. Information Collection and Use

2.1 Types of Data Collected

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, company information
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent on pages
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience

2.2 Purpose of Collection

We collect personal data to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Send administrative information
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Analyze and improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to external websites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not addressed to anyone under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete such information promptly.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Lawful Basis for Processing

Under GDPR and similar data protection laws, we process your personal data only on the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: Where you have explicitly provided consent for specific processing activities (e.g., marketing communications)
  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to perform our contract with you or to take steps at your request prior to entering a contract
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required to comply with applicable laws and regulatory requirements
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as improving our services, maintaining security, and fraud prevention
  • Vital Interests: Processing necessary to protect vital interests of you or another person

8. Data Retention Schedules

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Account Data: Retained for 3 years following account closure or termination of services
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 26 months from the date of collection
  • Marketing Consent Records: Retained until you withdraw your consent, then deleted within 30 days
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial and tax regulations
  • Support Communications: Retained for 2 years after resolution of inquiry

9. Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR and equivalent legislation, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You have the right to obtain a copy of your personal data and information about how we process it
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format for transfer to another service provider
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your data for direct marketing and certain other purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making: You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing that have legal effects

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer (see contact details below).

10. International Data Transfers

If your personal data is transferred outside your country of residence (particularly outside the European Economic Area), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC): We use EU Standard Contractual Clauses as approved by the European Commission to govern international transfers of personal data
  • Adequacy Decisions: Where available, we rely on Commission adequacy decisions for transfers to certain countries
  • Supplementary Measures: We implement additional technical and organizational measures to protect data integrity during international transfers

For details about specific transfer mechanisms applicable to your data, please contact our Data Protection Officer.
