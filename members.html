QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my membership tier?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your tier at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there a cancellation fee?

No. You can cancel anytime with no penalties or hidden fees. All billing is handled securely by Stripe.

What are MCP servers?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers are AI governance tools that integrate directly with AI assistants like Claude, ChatGPT, and others. Each server handles a specific compliance or safety domain. Browse the full catalog.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual memberships include a 20% discount. Contact memberships@CSGA-global.org to switch to annual billing.

How do I get invoices?

All members receive automatic invoices via Stripe. Contact our membership team at memberships@CSGA-global.org for custom billing needs.
