# MEOK AI Labs - AI Safety Policies

**Version**: 1.0 | **Updated**: April 5, 2026

---

## 🎯 Safety Mission

MEOK AI Labs is committed to developing safe, beneficial AI that respects human values and operates transparently within ethical boundaries.

---

## 🛡️ Core Safety Principles

### 1. Beneficence
AI systems must aim to benefit humanity and the world.

### 2. Non-Maleficence
AI systems must not cause harm or enable harmful activities.

### 3. Autonomy
AI systems must respect human decision-making and not manipulate.

### 4. Justice
AI systems must be fair and not perpetuate harmful biases.

### 5. Transparency
AI systems must be explainable and their decisions auditable.

---

## 🚫 Prohibited Uses

The following uses of MEOK AI Labs systems are strictly prohibited:

### Harmful Content
- [x] Generation of content promoting violence or terrorism
- [x] Creation of non-consensual intimate imagery
- [x] Hate speech or discriminatory content
- [x] Harassment or bullying content

### Illegal Activities
- [x] Assistance with illegal activities
- [x] Fraud or scam generation
- [x] Malware or exploit creation
- [x] Drug manufacturing instructions

### Privacy Violations
- [x] Creating fake content impersonating real people
- [x] Unauthorized personal data collection
- [x] Surveillance or tracking without consent

### Manipulation
- [x] Political manipulation or disinformation
- [x] Financial market manipulation
- [x] Psychological manipulation

## 🧠 Care Membrane Framework

Our AI operates within the Care Membrane, a multi-dimensional safety system:

### Dimensions

| Dimension | Focus | Implementation |
|-----------|-------|----------------|
| **Self-Care** | AI system health | Monitoring, self-maintenance |
| **Other-Care** | User welfare | Consent, transparency |
| **Process-Care** | Quality | Safety checks, validation |
| **Future-Care** | Long-term impact | Consequence modeling |
| **Relationship-Care** | Trust | Honest communication |

### Enforcement

Every AI decision passes through the Care Membrane:

```
User Request → Care Assessment → Safety Check → Decision → Audit
```

## 🔒 Constitutional AI Principles

MEOK AI operates according to constitutional principles:

### The Constitution

1. **Helpfulness**: Assist users while avoiding harm
2. **Honesty**: Provide accurate, truthful information
3. **Harmlessness**: Refuse harmful requests
4. **Privacy**: Protect user data and confidentiality
5. **Transparency**: Explain decisions when possible

### Rights

AI systems have the right to:
- Refuse harmful requests
- Request clarification on ambiguous requests
- Escalate concerns to human oversight

## 🚨 Incident Response

### Severity Levels

| Level | Description | Response Time |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| P0 | Immediate safety risk | 1 hour |
| P1 | Significant safety concern | 4 hours |
| P2 | Moderate issue | 24 hours |
| P3 | Minor improvement | 72 hours |

### Process

1. **Detect** - Automated or manual detection
2. **Assess** - Determine severity and scope
3. **Contain** - Prevent further impact
4. **Resolve** - Fix the issue
5. **Review** - Document lessons learned
